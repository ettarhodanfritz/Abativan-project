Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Abativan and our wheelchair accessible vehicles.

What types of wheelchair accessible vans do you offer?

We offer a wide range of conversions on popular vans such as Toyota Sienna, Honda Odyssey, Chrysler Pacifica, Ford Transit, and RAM Promaster. Options include rear entry, side entry, commercial vans, and both power or manual ramp types.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we provide in-house financing and collaborate with several banks to cater to a variety of credit scores. Our goal is to make wheelchair van ownership easy and affordable.

Can I purchase a van online?

Absolutely! You can view our inventory, request info, and even arrange delivery. Our online purchasing process is designed to be transparent and convenient.

Do you sell pre-owned vans?

Yes, we have a selection of high-quality used and pre-owned vans, all reconditioned to our industry-leading standards.
