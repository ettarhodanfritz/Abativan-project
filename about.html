About Abativan

Learn more about our mission, vision, and commitment to accessible vehicles.

Our Mission

Abativan exists to provide wheelchair accessible vehicles that empower independence, mobility, and confidence. We are committed to fair pricing, quality products, and exceptional service for every customer.

Our Vision

We aim to be the leading provider of mobility solutions, offering a wide range of accessible vehicles and supporting services that cater to the needs of individuals, families, and caregivers nationwide.

Why Choose Abativan?

  • High-quality wheelchair accessible vehicles.
  • Transparent pricing and financing options.
  • Expert guidance for choosing the right vehicle.
  • Trusted by thousands of satisfied customers.

Our Story

Founded with the goal of improving mobility for all, Abativan has served over 17,000 customers across the country. We combine decades of experience with personalized service to ensure every customer drives away satisfied.
