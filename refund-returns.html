Refund & Returns Policy

At Abativan, customer satisfaction is our top priority. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a clear and fair refund and returns policy.

Refunds

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after the returned vehicle or product has been inspected and approved. The refund will be issued to the original payment method.

Returns

You may return any vehicle or product purchased from Abativan within 14 days of delivery, provided it is in the same condition as received. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.

Conditions

  • Vehicle must be in original condition, without damage or modifications.
  • All original paperwork and accessories must be included.
  • Return shipping or pickup may incur additional fees depending on location.

For any questions regarding refunds or returns, please contact us.
